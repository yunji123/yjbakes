Pricing
Cakes are priced between £20–£25. The base price starts at £20 for a classic cake, and goes up to £25 for layered cakes, vegan, gluten-free, or allergy-friendly options. Get in touch and I'll give you an exact quote based on your order.
📦
Packaging
Every cake is carefully placed in a dedicated cake box to keep it secure and fresh. Your cake will arrive looking exactly as it should — ready to enjoy or present as a gift.
📅
Pre-order only
All cakes are made to order so they're as fresh as possible for you. Please get in touch in advance to place your order and we'll agree on a date that works for both of us.
🚗
Collection & delivery
Collection is free — just come pick it up! I also offer free delivery for nearby locations. For areas further away, a small delivery fee may apply depending on the distance. Message me and we can sort something out. 😊
Meet the baker
Yunji
Hi everyone :) I stress bake!

It started as a way to unwind from my stress at uni and during the pandemic. A few years and many bakes later I was baking for friends and family for birthdays and special occasions. Their joy and encouragement inspired me to share my passion with the wider community.

I prefer hand-made bakes because I put a lot of care into each one. I like tailoring it to each person using quality, healthier ingredients. To me, they feel more personal and meaningful than store-bought ones. If you love handmade cakes as much as I do, shoot me a message. I'd love to bake for you! 🎂❤️
More about Yunji
Originally from Seoul 🇰🇷, grew up in the Philippines 🇵🇭, and have called London home for almost a decade. Baking and sports are my two biggest passions! 😄
